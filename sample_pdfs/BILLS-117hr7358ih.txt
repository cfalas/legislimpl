                                 A BILL


 
 To amend the Federal Food, Drug, and Cosmetic Act to apply the exempt 
infant formula requirements to certain human donor milk, and for other 
                               purposes.

    Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the 
United States of America in Congress assembled,

SECTION 1. SHORT TITLE.

    This Act may be cited as the ``Donor Milk Safety Act''.

SEC. 2. APPLICATION OF EXEMPT INFANT FORMULA REQUIREMENTS TO CERTAIN 
              HUMAN DONOR MILK.

    Section 412(h) of the Federal Food, Drug, and Cosmetic Act (21 
U.S.C. 350a(h)) is amended by adding at the end the following:
    ``(3) Effective beginning one year after the date of the enactment 
of this paragraph, the exemption under paragraph (1) shall apply with 
respect to human milk which has undergone any bioburden reduction 
process in the same manner as such exemption applies with respect to 
infant formula described in such paragraph. Not later than one year 
after such date of enactment, the Secretary shall make such revisions 
as may be necessary to the regulations prescribed by the Secretary 
under paragraph (2) to carry out the preceding sentence.''.
