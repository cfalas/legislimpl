                                 A BILL


 
To authorize the Gateway Arch in St. Louis, Missouri, to be illuminated 
            by blue and yellow lights in support of Ukraine.

    Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the 
United States of America in Congress assembled,

SECTION 1. SHORT TITLE.

    This Act may be cited as the ``Gateway Solidarity Act''.

SEC. 2. ILLUMINATION OF THE GATEWAY ARCH IN SUPPORT OF UKRAINE.

    To show support and solidarity with the Ukrainian people, the 
Secretary of the Interior shall authorize the Gateway Arch in St. 
Louis, Missouri, to be illuminated by blue and yellow lights for a 
certain period of time, to be designated by the Secretary of the 
Interior.
