                                 A BILL


 
 To designate the facility of the United States Postal Service located 
    at 129 Walnut Street in Corning, New York, as the ``Amory `Amo' 
                 Houghton, Jr. Post Office Building''.

    Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the 
United States of America in Congress assembled,

SECTION 1. AMORY ``AMO'' HOUGHTON, JR. POST OFFICE BUILDING.

    (a) Designation.--The facility of the United States Postal Service 
located at 129 Walnut Street in Corning, New York, shall be known and 
designated as the ``Amory `Amo' Houghton, Jr. Post Office Building''.
    (b) References.--Any reference in a law, map, regulation, document, 
paper, or other record of the United States to the facility referred to 
in subsection (a) shall be deemed to be a reference to the ``Amory 
`Amo' Houghton, Jr. Post Office Building''.
