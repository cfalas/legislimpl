                                 A BILL


 
     To amend title 49, United States Code, to encourage economic 
   sustainability of intercity passenger rail service, and for other 
                               purposes.

    Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the 
United States of America in Congress assembled,

SECTION 1. SHORT TITLE.

    This Act may be cited as the ``Returning Amtrak To Economic 
Sustainability Act'' or the ``RATES Act''.

SEC. 2. AMTRAK MISSION AND GOALS.

    (a) Findings.--Section 24101(a)(5) of title 49, United States Code, 
is amended by striking ``Modern'' and inserting ``Economically 
sustainable''.
    (b) Mission.--Section 24101(b) of title 49, United States Code, is 
amended by inserting ``, while ensuring route profitability 
proportional to the Federal share of investment,'' before ``consisting 
of high quality''.
    (c) Goals.--Section 24101(c)(1) of title 49, United States Code, is 
amended by striking ``use its best business judgment in acting to''.
