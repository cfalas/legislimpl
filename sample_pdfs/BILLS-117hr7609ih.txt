                                 A BILL


 
  To prohibit the appeal of the decision issued by the United States 
District Court for the Middle District of Florida determining that the 
           mask mandate is unlawful, and for other purposes.

    Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the 
United States of America in Congress assembled,

SECTION 1. PROHIBITING APPEAL.

    No Federal funds may be used by the Department of Justice or any 
other Federal agency to appeal the decision filed on April 18, 2022, in 
the United States District Court for the Middle District of Florida, in 
Health Freedom Defense Fund Inc., et al. v. Biden, et al., 8:21-cv-
1693-KKM-AEP.
