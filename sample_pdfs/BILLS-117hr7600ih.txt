                                 A BILL


 
 To prohibit the Transportation Security Administration from using the 
``X'' gender designation in the TSA PreCheck advanced security program, 
                        and for other purposes.

    Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the 
United States of America in Congress assembled,

SECTION 1. SHORT TITLE.

    This Act may be cited as the ``Traditional Screening Application 
Act'' or the ``TSA Act''.

SEC. 2. PROHIBITION.

    The Administrator of the Transportation Security Administration may 
not use the ``X'' gender designation in the TSA PreCheck advanced 
security program. The Administrator may permit applicants to and 
participants in such program to select from only ``Male'' or 
``Female''.
