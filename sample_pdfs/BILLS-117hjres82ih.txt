                            JOINT RESOLUTION


 
Disapproving of the rule submitted by the Department of Justice and the 
 Department of Homeland Security relating to ``Procedures for Credible 
Fear Screening and Consideration of Asylum, Withholding of Removal, and 
              CAT Protection Claims by Asylum Officers''.

    Resolved by the Senate and House of Representatives of the United 
States of America in Congress assembled, That Congress disapproves the 
rule submitted by the Department of Justice and the Department of 
Homeland Security relating to ``Procedures for Credible Fear Screening 
and Consideration of Asylum, Withholding of Removal, and CAT Protection 
Claims by Asylum Officers'' (87 FR 18078), and such rule shall have no 
force or effect.
