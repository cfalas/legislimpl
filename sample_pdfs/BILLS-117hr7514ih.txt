                                 A BILL


 
 To designate the facility of the United States Postal Service located 
at 345 South Main Street in Butler, Pennsylvania, as the ``Andrew Gomer 
                    Williams Post Office Building''.

    Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the 
United States of America in Congress assembled,

SECTION 1. ANDREW GOMER WILLIAMS POST OFFICE BUILDING.

    (a) Designation.--The facility of the United States Postal Service 
located at 345 South Main Street in Butler, Pennsylvania, shall be 
known and designated as the ``Andrew Gomer Williams Post Office 
Building''.
    (b) References.--Any reference in a law, map, regulation, document, 
paper, or other record of the United States to the facility referred to 
in subsection (a) shall be deemed to be a reference to the ``Andrew 
Gomer Williams Post Office Building''.
